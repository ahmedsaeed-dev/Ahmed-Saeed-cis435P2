2 Credit Hours, Fall 2019
Lectures: Monday, 11:00 AM - 1:45 PM, PEC 1330

Course Description:

This course continues the work on practical software engineering principles and professional practice from CIS 4951/61/71/81. Purpose of course is to give students the opportunity to sharpen their software analysis and client communications skills. Students will continue working with real-world clients and taking a software project from conception to delivery of the product to the client. Classroom activities focus on student directed discussions of current profession and ethics issues,and presentations based on project milestones. This course is the second semester of an 8-month capstone experience. Students taking this course must have taken CIS 4951/61/71/81 in Summer 2019. An acceptance letter from the project client is needed to receive a grade in this course.
